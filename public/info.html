What is Sketch Lab?

Sketch Lab is a user interface (UI) sketching tool. You can use it to help you plan your projects. You can see some of it's cool features here:

Easy Drag-n-Drop and Resizing of Widgets

With Sketch Lab, it is easy to design user interfaces by dragging and dropping widgets to exactly where you want them. Do you need to change the size of a widget? No problem! Just drag the bottom-right corner to the size you want.

Sign in With Your Google Account

With Sketch Lab, there are no signup forms to fill out and there are no extra passwords to remember. Instead, you just sign in with your Google account, and Sketch Lab will automatically create an account for you behind the scenes.
